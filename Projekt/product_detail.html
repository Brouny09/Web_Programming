About the brand

Napapijri is an Italian premium casual-wear brand known for its distinctive blend of style and functionality. Established in 1987 in the Aosta Valley, Napapijri draws its inspiration from the extreme conditions of the North and the Finnish word "Napapiiri," which means the Arctic Circle. This influence is evident in the brand's iconic logo, which features a Norwegian flag, a tribute to explorers and adventurers. Napapijri's clothing and accessories are designed to withstand the elements, making them suitable for outdoor enthusiasts and urban explorers alike. The brand's product range includes jackets, parkas, sweaters, and a variety of accessories, all crafted with a keen eye for detail and quality. Napapijri has gained a reputation for its commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. They strive to reduce their ecological footprint by using eco-friendly materials and sustainable production practices. With a unique blend of fashion-forward design and a respect for nature, Napapijri has become a global symbol of adventure and style, offering individuals the opportunity to express their love for the great outdoors while looking effortlessly fashionable.
